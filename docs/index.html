This is Daydream.

Daydream is a casual, focused voxel survival game by Daniel P H Fox.

Dig resources from the ground. Build out your base. Rinse, repeat.

The engine is actively under development, and I'd love to show it to you.

Your dream worlds.

Explore an infinite world full of exciting terrain and impossibly deep caves - tuned to be awesome. Live the adventures you've always wanted to play.

Grow from simple roots.

Plant your stake with an ever-evolving base of operations. Use a creative range of workstations to unfold multiple engaging crafting progressions.

Think on your feet.

Everything interacts. Use blocks, material interactions, and physics to master environmental hazards and navigate a world that's larger than you.

Shape your creations.

Use smaller, more detailed blocks to build anything you can imagine. Paint the world in coloured lighting that realistically mixes with every surface.
